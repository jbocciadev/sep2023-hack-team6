About Us

Company History

BusyB came to be in 2022 from the collective minds of some CodeInstitute allumni. The idea was to give a platfrom to companies looking to show off all their great work towards sustainability.

What We Do

We provide a platform for enviromentally focused companies to share what they have in place to reduce their carbon footprint. We hope this will encourage as many companies as possible to up their game when it comes to their enviromental impact on the world. Register your company with us and you will be added to our interactive map and display proudly your efforts in making the world a better place.
